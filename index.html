About Us

We are witnessing a paradigm shift in the in the Information Technology domain driven by the fourth industrial revolution and there are enormous amount of opportunities for both job seekers as well as employers in emerging technologies. While job seekers have huge number of options in terms of well paying jobs, the employers also have great opportunities to acquire the right candidates from a large talent pool who can nurture the growth of their organization.

We are acting as a bridge in this ecosystem between the deserving candidates and companies providing best opportunities for growth and a great environment at the workplace. We are a preferred destination for companies looking for talent acquisition ranging from developers to top level executives. We specialize in mapping the requirements of the position floated by a firm accurately with the skillset of the jobseekers. The conventional process of hiring is very random and leads to unnecessary time wastage both on the part of company officials including HR managers and team leads as well as the candidates seeking opportunities. We have a very streamlined process and follow state-of-the-art practices for selecting the right candidate for a position based not only on the technical skills but also on the communication skills and aptitude.

We focus on delivering innovative solutions to attract the best talent in the market and the quality of our work can be judged by the fact that 90% of our business comes from referrals and repeat clients.

Hiring Process

Our USP lies in our hiring process wherein we minutely observe the skillset of the candidates and map it with the job requirements. To achieve this, we have a panel of highly experienced industry professionals as well as experts from leading academic institutions across the country serving as consultants who screen the right candidate for a target opening. While industry professionals test a candidate’s capability based on his experience and acquired skillset for the job requirements, the pool of academicians judge the fundamental knowledge of the candidate alongwith the aptitude of the candidate. We further have a dedicated team of HR professionals to select the best candidates based on their communication skills which is of paramount importance in today’s rapidly changing technological environment.
